About Us


Simple E-Store Templates for a Growing Company

This templated website can easily be turned into your own e-store to quickly get your content out to your customers.

I have years of experience creating secure websites that allow for easy data manipulation by incorping RESTful data endpoints across multiple Amazon services to keep your content both secure and to store it at a low cost.

Features and Services involved:
  • Uses DynamoDB that can handle any small business needs for fast and free storage
  • Amazon Cognito for secure admin access to easily manage all of your data
  • Amazon Lambda used for a serverless architecture to perform backend tasks such and email notifications and dynamoDB data retrieval
  • Display your store content in an eligant and easy to see layout using the front end Bootstrap framework
  • Add support for a credit payment system through Authorize.net
  • Keep track of your stock and availability through the admin interface
